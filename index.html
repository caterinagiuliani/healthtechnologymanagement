Afya Moja Project

The Advanced Skill Course is part of the broader framework of the Afya Moja Project. Afya Moja (Swahili for ‚ÄúOne Health‚Äù) aims to strengthen African capacities in the design, assessment, maintenance, commercialization, and disposal of health technologies.

Objective 3: Short-Term Training Programs

At least six short-term training programs will be organized both in Italy and Africa:
